Spotify + Anki helps you learn to recognize the artist of a given song. It generates Anki cards which trigger playback through Spotify.

How this app works

This app uses the concept of a "tested artist": this is an artist that you have decided you'd like to be familiar with. Once you add a selection of such artists, the app will automatically generate cards from the top 3 most-popular songs from their profile page, as well as any songs that you've liked from that artist.

How to import new songs into Anki

Download the latest set of songs (be patient, this may take up to a minute). Open the file, and your new cards will appear under a deck called "Spotify Tracks."

How to add a new artist

While using the Spotify app, search for the artist you'd like to test for, go to their artist page, and click follow (see below for a screenshot). Then, back on the homepage, add the artist to the set of Tested Artists.
